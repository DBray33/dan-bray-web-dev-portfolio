About Me

I’m Dan Bray, a motivated and detail-oriented professional with a passion for creativity and technology. After 8 years of excelling in the craft beer industry, I’m transitioning my skills and experience into a career in web development and digital design. Throughout my career, I’ve honed my abilities in project management, web and graphic design, social media strategy, and e-commerce development. At Broken Chair Brewery, I managed every facet of operations, from crafting award-winning beer recipes to spearheading digital marketing campaigns. My efforts elevated the brewery to become a community favorite and the county’s “Best Craft Brewery” every year the award was available (they got rid of it).

Beyond brewing, I designed all visual branding, including can labels, apparel, and merchandise, while also managing the brewery’s website and social media presence. In addition to my brewery career, I’ve built successful e-commerce stores and provided freelance graphic design services. These ventures allowed me to design and market custom merchandise across platforms like Shopify, Etsy, and eBay, where my designs achieved top search rankings. I’m currently advancing my web development skills through certifications and hands-on projects. My portfolio showcases my commitment to crafting impactful digital solutions while enhancing my proficiency in tools such as the MERN stack and WordPress, and reflects my passion for building custom-coded websites.

Development Approach

In every project, my approach to web development is rooted in precision, creativity, and a deep commitment to delivering websites that drive engagement, enhance user experience, and achieve measurable success. Whether it's for my work-in-progress freelance website, or for a client, the ultimate goal is to attract new customers, provide a seamless experience that fosters lasting connections, and ultimately drive increased sales. Transitioning from a career managing the complexities of the craft beer industry to web development, I have honed a meticulous attention to detail, a problem-solving mindset, and an unwavering focus on user satisfaction. I am obsessed with creating movements and functionalities that make sense, feel intuitive, and are inherently satisfying—whether it's scroll behavior, hover characteristics, or ensuring elements adapt seamlessly and beautifully as the viewport changes. Valley Peak Roofing

A comprehensive website redesign and content restructure for Valley Peak Roofing, focused on maximizing SEO performance and user engagement. The project involved migrating from WordPress to custom-coded architecture, allowing for complete control over performance optimization and functionality.

The site features extensive SEO implementation with Core Web Vitals tracking, integrated Google Tag Manager and GA4 analytics, SEMrush monitoring, and Google Search Console optimization. Interactive elements include strategically placed modals throughout the site – informative overlays for service details, streamlined contact forms for quick inquiries, and dedicated financing application forms to facilitate customer conversions.

All form submissions are efficiently handled through Netlify's backend services. Since deployment and indexing, the site has shown substantial and consistent growth in search impressions, demonstrating the effectiveness of the SEO-focused redesign and technical improvements in the site's visibility and organic reach.

Waldman Electric

A clean, professional website redesign for Waldman Electric, a trusted local electrician company. The project involved migrating from their previous Hibu platform to a modern, custom-built solution that better represents their brand and services.

While maintaining a straightforward approach appropriate for a local service business, the new design focuses on improved user experience, clear service presentation, and easy customer contact options. Form submissions are seamlessly processed through Netlify's infrastructure, ensuring reliable lead capture and customer communication.

Following deployment and proper indexing, the site has experienced substantial and consistent growth in search impressions within the first few months, significantly improving the company's online visibility in their local market and helping them connect with more potential customers in need of electrical services.

Property Management Group

This is a full, 22 page, comprehensive website for a property management company. It offers services for clients (real estate owners) and the tenants renting their properties.

In order to display information in different sections without creating information and content bloat, I've place information on the back of cards that flip when you hover your cursor over them, which is replaced by a tap on mobile.

There is a bar chart that displays the average monthly rent in the area, and when you scroll to the section, the bars dynamically raise to their respective rent price with the use of javascript functionality. It's very aesthetically pleaseing when scrollig to the section.

The site has a listings section. If I utilized this template for a client, I would implement full rental listing pages, fit with photos, descriptions, all the details you would find on a property listing on a platform like Zillow.

I plan on developing a few different templates for property management companies. The goal for this one is highly responsive and easily navigable. Whether you are a tenant or owner, current or prospective, you should be able to find what you're looking for on this site with ease.

Local Borough Photography & Community Event Website

A proposal website for the beloved local photography and community group, Berks County Views. The site opens with an auto-rotating carousel showcasing their stunning regional photography that immediately connects visitors to the beauty of Berks County.

Below the carousel, an upcoming events section keeps the community informed about opportunities to gather and celebrate local culture. The centerpiece photography display features an interactive gallery where a main featured image is flanked by six smaller thumbnails – hovering over any thumbnail dynamically updates the main display, creating an engaging browsing experience.

Each photography collection includes a link to a dedicated gallery page with complete event coverage. The site rounds out with sections for local news, photography tips, and a streamlined contact form – all presented in a responsive design that works beautifully across devices, reflecting the group's commitment to making Berks County's visual heritage accessible to everyone.

Brewing Company Redesign

A modern, immersive website redesign for a craft brewery featuring dynamic parallax scrolling in the hero section. As visitors explore the site, content elements transition smoothly into view through subtle animations triggered by scrolling.

The design showcases the brewery's craft beer selection with appealing visuals and descriptions, highlights upcoming events, and includes an intuitive online ordering system. A streamlined contact form makes customer communication effortless.

The fully responsive layout ensures a seamless experience across all devices, with a clean aesthetic that puts the focus on the brewery's products while creating a satisfying, engaging scrolling journey that reflects the craft and care of the brewing process.
